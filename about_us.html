WHO WE ARE

Momentous Click was created by a group of young students of Chittagong, lead by Md. Meherab Hossain who chose their passion as their profession and this incident of their life have been pushing them constantly to expand their potentiality. From 2015 we have covered more than 1300+ events. Our team is now a strong team of Photographers, cinematographers & Editors. All our photographers are highly educated & Professional. Our sole endeavor lies in creating images that would meet our thrust for creativity and taste for excellence.
Each of our photographers shares a similar passion for artistic photography and has their unique skillset. Our photographers, already popular for their amiable nature, are expert in making their sessions fun and let their subjects be themselves, express their emotions comfortably and enjoy the ride. We believe, the appreciation and gratitude we receive from our clients and well-wishers are our greatest achievements.
